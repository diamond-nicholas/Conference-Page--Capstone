BRAND PARTNERSHIP & ACTIVATION

Klausified Music is one of Africa’s leading entertainment companies. Founded in 2012 as a record label by legendary Afrobeats producer, Don Jazzy, Mavin Records produced some of the definitive music and artistes such as Wande Cole, Tiwa Savage, and Reekado Banks. Currently, its roster spans a variety of genres with artists including Korede Bello, Johnny Drille, Di'ja, D'Prince, Dr. SID, LadiPoe, DNA, Rema, Crayon and DJ Big N. As the digital era continues to refashion the way we do things, Mavin’s taste for delivering the utmost value has inspired the company’s expansion into innovative services such as partnerships, content production, publishing and marketing. Today, Mavin is a community of amazing people dedicated to creativity, innovation and breaking new frontiers.
